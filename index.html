Process

Five stages.
No surprises.

The same sequence for every vehicle, so you always know which stage your job card is sitting in.

01

Drop-off & intake

Describe the symptom, hand over the keys, and we open a job card with your vehicle's history attached.

02

Diagnosis

Computer scan, road test and physical inspection. Nothing gets touched until we know exactly what's wrong.

03

Itemised quote

Parts and labour broken out separately, sent to your phone. You approve before we order or repair anything.

04

Repair

Carried out by the same mechanic who ran the diagnosis — not handed off down the line.

05

Quality check & handover

Road-tested, cleaned, and the job card closed out with everything we found and fixed.
